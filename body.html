Body Positive

Body Positive | | was originally inspired by the LA Fitness App. The idea was to redesign this app creating better usability and a simpler UI. I designed a fitness app that shared all the characteristics inside a fitness app, consisting of workouts, logging meals, finding classes and creating schedules to best fit your lifestyle.

Using UX research, I conducted online surveys to study the psychology behind making a simple all-in-one fitness app. Creating journey maps and site maps to build out the emotional and architectural structure of the app.

Created as a final project for my UX Design course at Beach Coders, a certification course, in Los Angeles, CA.
